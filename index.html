4 · Regulatory surveillance

FDA labels — structured product labelling

openFDA drug/label returned 212 SPL records containing ascorbic acid as a substance. Boxed warnings present on the sampled records are driven by co-formulants — omega-3 bleeding risk on prenatal DHA products, and standard paediatric iron-overdose warning on iron-containing multivitamins. No boxed warning is attributable to ascorbate itself. Typical ascorbate-related label content is benign: allergic rash / sensitisation, hypersensitivity with specific formulations.

FDA enforcement — 14 recalls, 2021-04-24 to 2026-04-24

Dominant pattern: compounding-pharmacy sterility failures on IV ascorbic acid. One Class I (glass particulates) and one Class III (pouch leakage on a combination bowel prep).
